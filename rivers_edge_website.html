Our Story

My roots run deep in the Quad Cities. For the first ten years of my life, I lived on a farm, where the outdoors simply became a part of me, and a well-kept lawn felt like a natural extension of home. What inspired me to start my own business was the unmatched work ethic demonstrated by my father, who owned and operated successful businesses. Additionally, my stepfather, a certified arborist, showed me the importance of combining that work ethic with specialized knowledge. And, I must give credit to my mother, whose absolute love for nature, animals, and all things green was a constant source of inspiration. Her 'magical touch' with gardening, trees, and especially flowers, showed me the beauty, importance, and pride in nurturing what surrounds you.

Growing up in the Quad City area, I remember Sunday mornings, the sound of lawnmowers, the sense of community as everyone took pride in their homes and lawns. Majestic oaks and vibrant green lawns weren't just scenery; they represented the pride in a well-kept home and a well-kept lawn, a feeling that resonated throughout the entire neighborhood. It wasn't just about trees and grass; it was about the feeling of home, right here in the Quad Cities.

Seeing the impact my parents and stepfather had on their surroundings and community, I decided to dedicate myself to learning the best practices in arboriculture and landscaping. To further enhance my expertise, I am currently studying to become a certified arborist, ensuring that River's Edge always provides the most knowledgeable and up-to-date care. I started helping neighbors, and quickly realized this wasn't just a job, it was a way to truly serve my community.

That's how River's Edge was born. It's built on a foundation of integrity, expertise, and a genuine love for the outdoors. We take immense pride in our craftsmanship, ensuring every tree is trimmed with precision, and every lawn is cared for with pride. Our greatest satisfaction comes from seeing the smiles on our customers' faces, knowing we've helped enhance the beauty and safety of their homes.

As a local family-owned business, we're deeply invested in the well-being of our community. We strive to not only provide exceptional service but also build lasting relationships with our clients. At River's Edge, we adhere to the highest level of customer satisfaction, prioritizing the health and safety of our clients. We're not just maintaining landscapes; we're nurturing the heart of the Quad Cities, one yard at a time. That's a commitment we take pride in.
